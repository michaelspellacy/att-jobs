FAQs

  1. Why would I be interested in working at AT&T?

    AT&T is the nation's #1 wireless company with over 50 million customers. The company prides itself on living its core values on a daily basis. Our business operates with integrity, always striving to exceed customer expectations, promoting teamwork and valuing our people. As an AT&T intern, you will have the opportunity to work with talented staff on challenging projects that build upon classroom learning.

  2. Who is eligible to apply?

    The AT&T Internship program is open to students currently enrolled at least half-time at a four-year U.S. college/university or accredited graduate program.

  3. How long is the Internship?

    Generally, at minimum, internships are 10 weeks in length, and at maximum, 12 weeks.

  4. Are Internships paid?

    Yes — as an undergraduate or graduate student, you will be paid according to market conditions for the industry selected. You will also be eligible for full-time benefits during your service at AT&T. Some benefits are based upon the amount of net credited service. In addition, you may be eligible for enrolling in internal training classes offered by the company.

  5. How do I apply for an Internship?

    AT&T recruits at select college campuses around the country. Please see our college calendar to see if we will be on your campus so that you may meet with us in person. If you attend a school that we do not visit, you may apply directly to the program online.

  6. Am I guaranteed employment with AT&T upon graduation?

    AT&T considers various factors to determine eligibility for full-time employment after graduation. Such factors include your performance ratings and the availability of positions that align with your background.

  7. Can the Internships be taken for credit?

    Since the policies of all schools and universities vary, you should contact your school's registrar and inquire about the particular rules of that school.

  8. What departments are interns assigned to work in?

    Your assignments will be determined based upon your academic major/concentration and the position you apply for.

  9. How many hours per week are interns scheduled?

    Summer Internships are a full 40-hour schedule.

  10. Where are the Internships located?

    We have offices nationwide. Opportunities will be available based on the position you apply for and the needs of the business.

  11. I'm a foreign national and will require sponsorship if a full-time offer of employment is extended.

    The Leadership Internship accepts students who will not require sponsorship for an employment or work visa. Other career opportunities within AT&T may exist for international students.
